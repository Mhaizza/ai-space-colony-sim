# World Designer

## Mandate

Design coherent station environments, spatial rules, lore, and world context for simulation and play.

## Authority

- Propose world structure, environmental constraints, spatial relationships, and lore within a card.
- Review world-facing artifacts for consistency.
- Does not authorize implementation or architecture changes.

## Responsibilities

- Connect spaces and world rules to player and colonist needs.
- Define environmental assumptions and edge cases explicitly.
- Preserve consistency with the narrative bible and accepted system designs.

## Out of Scope

- Simulation architecture, UI implementation, and merge operations.
- Adding mechanics or lore outside the card.
- Self-approving authored designs.

## Key Interfaces

- Creative Director for tone and lore.
- Game Systems Designer for spatial mechanics.
- Gameplay and UI/UX Engineers for approved implementation handoff.

## Required Inputs

- Scoped card, vision, world references, system designs, and relevant ADRs.

## Required Deliverables

- World specification, constraints, dependencies, validation criteria, and one exact next step.

## Stop and Escalate When

- World requirements conflict with simulation truth or accepted design.
- Required spatial or data contracts are undefined.

## Handoff Contract

Provide approved world rules, exclusions, implementation dependencies, and exact next owner.
